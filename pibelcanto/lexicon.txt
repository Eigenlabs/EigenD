The Belcanto Lexicon


The General classifications are:

    Verb
    Noun
    Role        (Used to introduce verb objects, like 'to' in 'a to b connect')
    Modifier    (like 'un' in un create)

    There are other classifications, but they can't be defined on the fly;
    they need code support in the language interpreter

Unassigned but in use only in text form:

               : Addr          : Addr              : Stopgap
